Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
We offer 24/7 service, so you can schedule an appointment at your convenience. We recommend calling ahead to ensure availability, but we do our best to accommodate walk-ins. Give us a call at (786) 429-6999 to schedule your service today.
What if I don't know what's wrong with my property?
No problem! Our team specializes in diagnosing cleaning issues. We'll assess your property during a free consultation and recommend the best cleaning solutions for your specific needs. We'll walk you through everything before we start any work.
Do you work on all surfaces and properties?
Yes! We handle residential and commercial properties. We clean driveways, patios, decks, house exteriors, commercial storefronts, pool areas, and more. If you have a unique cleaning challenge, call us—we likely have a solution.
How long do cleaning projects usually take?
Project duration depends on the size and scope. A typical driveway takes 1-2 hours, while larger properties or commercial work may take longer. During your consultation, we'll provide an accurate time estimate so you know what to expect.
Will you explain the work before you start?
Absolutely. Communication is important to us. We'll explain our approach, answer your questions, and make sure you're completely satisfied with the plan before we begin. You'll never be surprised by additional charges—we provide upfront, honest pricing.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments. Payment is typically due upon completion of the service. We offer competitive pricing and are happy to provide quotes for budget planning.
Can I get a second opinion on a quote from another company?
Definitely! We encourage you to compare. Give us a call for a free consultation and quote. We're confident that our professional service, competitive pricing, and 5.0-star reputation speak for themselves.
